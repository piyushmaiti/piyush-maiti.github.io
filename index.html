Bio

Welcome to my photography journey! I'm Piyush Maiti, a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing life's most beautiful and meaningful moments through my lens.

With years of experience across various photography genres, I specialize in street photography, landscapes, cityscapes, and experimental work. My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to create images that not only look stunning but also tell compelling stories.

I believe that every photograph should evoke emotion and preserve memories that last a lifetime. Whether it's the raw energy of street life, the serene beauty of landscapes, or the dynamic pulse of urban environments, I work to capture the essence of each moment.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new locations, studying light patterns, or experimenting with new techniques to continuously evolve my craft.
